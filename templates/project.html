About the Alberta Accountability Project

The Alberta Accountability Project is dedicated to promoting transparency, integrity, and accountability in Alberta's political and public spheres. Our mission is to provide a platform for informed discussion, investigative reporting, and community engagement to hold public officials and institutions accountable.

Our Goals

  • Expose corruption and mismanagement through in-depth investigations.
  • Amplify community voices and concerns about governance.
  • Educate the public on key issues affecting Alberta's future.
  • Foster a culture of openness and ethical leadership.

Our Approach

We combine rigorous journalism with community-driven insights, leveraging multimedia content to share stories that matter. From policy analysis to firsthand accounts, we aim to empower Albertans with the knowledge to drive change.

Join us in building a more accountable Alberta. Share your stories, insights, or concerns through our platform or contact us directly.
